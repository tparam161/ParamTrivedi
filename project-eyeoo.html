Eyeoo

Eyeoo is a professional eye-glasses brand. Mr. Nirav and their family is in the eye-wear business for the last 20 years. Now they are expanding their business across the whole country. Currently, they operate in some areas of India, but now expecting and trying to cover big cities across India and start their different franchise. The best thing about Eyeoo is that they have a very unique collection of glasses, and their customer service makes that even more helpful to explore all the collections. I visited some showrooms and took the experience of their glasses and lenses. I felt what customers look and expect into their glasses, after going through a lot of things I got this perfect design.
